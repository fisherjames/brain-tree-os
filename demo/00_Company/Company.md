# Company

**clsh.dev** (pronounced "clash") is an open-source browser-based terminal that gives you real shell access to your Mac from your phone.

## What We Do

We built a phone-first terminal experience. Not a dumbed-down mobile shell, but real zsh access with a UI designed for touch screens and small displays.

## How It Works

1. Run `npx clsh` on your Mac
2. Opens an ngrok tunnel to your machine
3. Access your terminal from any browser on any device
4. Full xterm.js terminal with mobile-optimized keyboard overlays
