# Vision & Mission

## Vision

**Your Mac, in your pocket.**

Access your development machine from anywhere. A real terminal, not a toy.

## Mission

Make remote terminal access effortless, secure, and phone-native. Remove every barrier between a developer and their machine.

## Principles

1. **Phone-first**: Every pixel designed for mobile
2. **No iOS keyboard**: Custom keyboard overlays replace Apple's keyboard entirely
3. **Zero friction**: `npx clsh` and you're connected
4. **Real terminals**: Full zsh/bash with job control, vim, tmux support
5. **Security by default**: Encrypted tunnels, session auth, rate limiting
6. **Open source**: MIT licensed, contributor-friendly, transparent

## Progression

- v1: Solo developer accessing their own Mac (current)
- v2: Team access with permissions and shared sessions
- v3: Cloud-hosted machines with managed infrastructure
