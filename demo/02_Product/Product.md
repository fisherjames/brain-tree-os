## Product Definition

clsh.dev is a phone-first browser terminal. The core insight: developers need terminal access from their phones, but mobile keyboards make it impossible. We solve this with custom keyboard overlays and a touch-native UI.

## Target Users

- Solo developers who want to check on deployments from their phone
- Developers who SSH into machines while away from their desk
- Anyone who needs quick terminal access without opening a laptop
