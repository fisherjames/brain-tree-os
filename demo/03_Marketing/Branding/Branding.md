# Branding

Visual identity and brand guidelines for clsh.dev.

## Brand Identity

- **Name**: clsh (pronounced "clash")
- **Domain**: clsh.dev
- **Tagline**: "Your Mac, in your pocket"

## Visual Language

### Logo
Terminal cursor icon (blinking block cursor) with "clsh" wordmark in monospace font.

### Colors
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Terminal Black | #1A1A2E | Primary background |
| Matrix Green | #00FF41 | Accent, cursor, CTAs |
| Soft White | #E0E0E0 | Body text |
| Accent Purple | #6366F1 | Interactive elements |

### Typography
- **Headings**: Inter
- **Body**: Inter
- **Code/Terminal**: JetBrains Mono

## Brand Voice
- Technical but approachable
- Hacker-friendly, no corporate speak
- Direct, concise, developer-native

Consistent across all [[Content]] and communications.
