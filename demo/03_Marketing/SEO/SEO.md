# SEO Strategy

Search engine optimization for clsh.dev organic discovery.

## Target Keywords

| Keyword | Volume | Difficulty | Priority |
|---------|--------|-----------|----------|
| mobile terminal | 1,200 | Medium | High |
| terminal from phone | 800 | Low | High |
| browser terminal | 2,400 | High | Medium |
| remote terminal access | 1,800 | High | Medium |
| npx terminal | 400 | Low | High |

## On-Page SEO

### Landing Page
- Title: "clsh.dev - Your Mac, in Your Pocket"
- H1: "Access Your Terminal from Your Phone"
- Meta: "Phone-first browser terminal with custom keyboards. Real shell access via npx clsh."

### Blog Posts
- Target long-tail keywords around mobile terminal usage
- Technical content for developer search queries
- Architecture posts for "how to build" queries

## Technical SEO

- Server-side rendering for crawlability
- Sitemap.xml and robots.txt configured
- Open Graph meta tags for social sharing
- Schema markup for software application

Supports [[Content]] discoverability and organic growth.
