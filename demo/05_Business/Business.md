## Business Model

### Current (v1): Open Source
- Free, MIT licensed
- Revenue: $0 (intentional)
- Goal: Build community and prove product-market fit

### Future (v2+): Freemium SaaS
- Free: Self-hosted (current functionality)
- Pro: Cloud machines, team access, persistent sessions
- Enterprise: SSO, audit logs, compliance
