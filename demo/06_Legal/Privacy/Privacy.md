# Privacy Policy

Data handling and privacy practices for clsh.dev.

## Core Principle

**We collect nothing.** clsh runs entirely on your machine. No data leaves your computer except through the encrypted tunnel you explicitly create.

## What We Don't Collect

- No analytics or tracking
- No terminal command logging
- No session recording
- No user accounts or personal information
- No cookies or local storage
- No third-party scripts

## Data Flow

```
Your Phone → HTTPS (ngrok) → Your Mac
```

All data stays between your devices. We never see, store, or process terminal data.

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Data Shared |
|---------|-------------|
| ngrok | Connection metadata only |
| Cloudflare | Standard web server logs |
| npm | Download counts only |
